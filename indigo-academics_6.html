Who we are

Tutoring built by students, for students.

Indigo Academics was founded by a group of high school students who believe the closest perspective to a struggling learner is someone who just worked through the same challenge. Our tutors are academically strong peers — and in place of pay, they earn leadership experience and verified volunteer service hours.

Every class happens online, making it easy for students and tutors to connect from anywhere. Revenue is split between charitable donations and growing the company so we can reach more students.

Session structure

Every class follows
a proven format.

Sessions run 1 hour (or 90 minutes for extended classes) and are held online. Each block is designed for focused, active learning — never filler.

15–20
min
Questions & Review
Open time for the student to ask questions about the previous session or current school material. Time can be extended if needed or shortened if there is nothing to review.
20
min
Teaching Session
The tutor delivers a focused lesson on a new concept using a prepared slideshow, with clear step-by-step explanations and worked examples.
15–20
min
Practice Problems
The student works through problems to apply what was just taught, demonstrating understanding and building confidence before independent work.
5–10
min
Wrap-Up & Homework
The tutor assigns a practice worksheet for before the next session, then sends a class summary to parents via email or WhatsApp covering concepts covered, homework assigned, and what comes next.

Policies

Clear expectations for everyone.

We keep things fair and transparent for students, parents, and tutors alike. These policies are designed to protect everyone's time.

Cancellation Policy
All classes must be canceled or rescheduled at least 24 hours before the scheduled start time. With sufficient notice, the session can be rescheduled at the student's choosing. Cancellations within the 24-hour window cannot be made up and are not eligible for a refund.
Tutor Consistency
We make every effort to assign the same tutor to each student consistently. When scheduling constraints require a different instructor, all tutors are trained and certified to teach at the same level of proficiency.
Parent Communication
Tutors are required to send a session summary to parents after every class via email or WhatsApp, covering concepts taught, practice assigned, and what will be covered in the next session.
Scheduling Limits
Students may schedule up to 2 regular classes per week. To request additional sessions beyond this, please contact our service center directly.
